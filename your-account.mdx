Keeping your personal data up to date is essential for security and proper identification on the Tess AI platform. The "Account" section within your settings is the central location for managing all your profile information.

This article shows you how to access and modify your personal data.

### **How to access the Account settings**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the account settings">
    Log in to your Tess AI account and then click on your profile icon, located in the lower left corner of the screen. In the menu that opens, select the "Settings" option.

    <Frame>
      ![Tessdocs Settingbottom](/images/tessdocs-settingbottom-4.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Account settings">
    By default, you will be directed to the "Account" tab. On this screen, you can manage the following information:

    1. Personal Data: Change your first name, last name, and profile picture.
    2. Access Email and Location: Update the email address associated with your account, as well as your location.

    <Frame>
      ![Tessdocs Config](/images/tessdocs-config-3.png)
    </Frame>
    3. Password Change: Create a new access password for your account.

    <Frame>
      ![Captura De Tela 2026 02 13 Às 15 17 45](/images/CapturadeTela2026-02-13às15.17.45-1.png)
    </Frame>
    4. Delete Account: Start the process to permanently delete your Tess AI account.

    <Frame>
      ![Captura De Tela 2026 02 13 Às 15 17 52](/images/CapturadeTela2026-02-13às15.17.52.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Danger>
  **<u>Important Warning about Account Deletion</u>**

  The action of deleting your account is permanent and irreversible. Upon confirmation, all your data, agents, history, and settings will be lost. All of your work will be gone. Make absolutely sure before proceeding with this action.
</Danger>
