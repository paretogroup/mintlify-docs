title: "System Status"
---

Tess AI provides a dedicated page where you can monitor the operational status of all our services in real time.

### **What is the System Status page?**

The "System Status" page is a centralized dashboard that provides information about the operability of the Tess AI platform and its integrated APIs. If any instability or scheduled maintenance occurs, this will be the first place to reflect that information.

![Tessdocs Status](/images/tessdocs-status.png)

### **How to access the Status page?**

> _1. Through the direct link_
>
> You can access the dashboard at any time at the following address: [<u>https://status.tessai.io/</u>](https://status.tessai.io/).
>
> _2. Through the Tess AI platform_
>
> You can also find the path to the status page within your Tess AI account. Follow the steps below:
>
> - Go to the "Settings" screen in the left side menu of the platform.
>
>   <img
>     src="/images/tessdocs-settingbottom-3.png"
>     alt="Tessdocs Settingbottom"
>     title="Tessdocs Settingbottom"
>     style={{ width:"38%" }}
>   />
> - In the settings menu, select the "Data Control" option.
> - Then, click on "System Status".
>
> ![Tessdocs Sytemstatus](/images/tessdocs-sytemstatus.png)

### **How to receive status updates?**

For your convenience, you can subscribe to receive automatic notifications about any changes in the status of our services.

On the status page, you will find an option to subscribe ("Subscribe to updates"). You can choose to receive all notifications or only those related to specific platform components that are most relevant to your use.

![Tessdocs Statussubs](/images/tessdocs-statussubs.png)

<Tip>
  **IMPORTANT TIP!**

  If the status page indicates that all systems are operational but you are still experiencing difficulties, it may be a specific issue with your account or configuration. In that case, do not hesitate to contact us.
</Tip>
