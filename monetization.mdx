### **Accessing your Monetization Dashboard**

To view your financial information on the platform, the path is simple and quick.

<Step title='Log in to your Tess AI account and then click your profile icon, located in the bottom-left corner of the screen. In the menu that opens, select the "Settings" option.'>
  Log in to your Tess AI account and then click your profile icon, located in the bottom-left corner of the screen. In the menu that opens, select the "Settings" option.
</Step>

<Step stepNumber={2} title='In the "Workspace" section, click Monetization'>
  In the "Workspace" section, click Monetization

  <Frame>
    ![Image](/images/image-36.png)
  </Frame>
</Step>

### **Understanding your Income Sources**

When you access the monetization area, you’ll find a detailed dashboard with your earnings organized by category:

- Subscriptions: Revenue generated from users who subscribe to your Workspace.
- Agent Sales: Earnings from the direct sale of your agents.
- Runs (executions): Accumulated value from other users running your agents.
